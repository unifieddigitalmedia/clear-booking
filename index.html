Reserve is a wordpress plugin developed by Unified Digital Media

Booking / Reservation system by Unified Digital Media is designed for travel accomodation provider that want to offer their guest more convenience when making a booking. The booking / reservation system allows travellers to choose their rooms as well as available promotional offers and amenities that would make their stay more comfortable. Whilst allow easy checkout using the PayPal API.

 

Reserve creates an easy to manage booking for guests as all their choices are bundled into one convient location and totalled, giving them full transparency of their booking. Using this WordPress Booking / Reservation plugin, lets travel accomodation providers you can add a places of interest filter and map on any page.

 

What Reserve can offer:

A new and exciting reservation system enabling itemised billing, promotions, amenties and easy to use PayPal checkout.

Here are just some of the plugins great features:

  • PayPal payment gateway
  • Stand alone reservation , booking details, booking confirmation , thank you and booking cancellation pages
  • Reservation form on anypage using wordpress shortcode
  • Automatic reservation dates validation
  • Itemised billing
  • Tax calculations and sub-totals including discounts and amenties

BOOKING FORM AND CALENDAR

Validated booking dates and one year calendar

ITEMISED BILLING

Transparent billing of rooms, offers and amentis choosen with built in tax calulations and sub totals.

PAY PAY GATEWAY

Easy to use , familar an convenient payment gateway using Pay Pal Api to accept debit and credit cards
